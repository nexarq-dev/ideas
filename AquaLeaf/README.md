# AquaLeaf™ — Water-Activated Natural Fibre Wipe

**Status:** Concept  
**Category:** Product Innovation / Sustainability / Personal Hygiene  
**Origin:** Nairobi, Kenya — May 2026

---

## The Problem

Standard tissues fall apart when wet. Wet wipes solve that — but require chemical preservatives, use non-biodegradable synthetic fibres, and create plastic waste. No mainstream product combines wet-wipe cleaning power with dry storage, natural materials, and zero preservatives.

---

## The Idea

AquaLeaf™ is a **dry-stored, water-activated natural fibre wipe**.

- Sold completely **dry** — no preservatives needed
- Consumer adds water at point of use — activates in 3–5 seconds
- Made from **natural, biodegradable fibres** (bamboo, cotton, lyocell)
- Packaged in **plastic-free** kraft paper or cardboard
- Fully **biodegrades** within 4–12 weeks after disposal

---

## Proposed Fibre Formulation

| Fibre | Proportion | Role |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Bamboo (lyocell/spunlace) | 60% | Primary — softness + absorption |
| Cotton (hydroentangled) | 30% | Structure — wet integrity |
| Lyocell | 10% | Performance — wet strength boost |

---

## Key Differentiators

- Zero preservatives or chemical additives
- 100% natural and biodegradable fibres
- Plastic-free packaging
- Indefinite shelf life (dry storage)
- Local sourcing potential (sisal, banana fibre — East Africa)
